Site Purpose

Prevent Forest Fires: Safeguard Our Future

The site is designed to raise awareness about the destructive effects of forest fires, with a focus on the common practice of slash-and-burn agriculture in the country. It will provide information on the causes of forest fires, their environmental impacts, and offer actionable steps to prevent them. The goal is to educate the public and encourage collective efforts to protect forests.

Color Schema

The website's color scheme is inspired by nature, particularly forests, to create an earthy and environmentally-conscious aesthetic. The following colors will be used:

#228B22: Forest green will be used for header and footer to create an immediate connection to nature.

#FFFFFF: White will be used for the background color to maintain a clean and modern design.

#8B4513: Brown will be used for accents to symbolize the earth and land.

Typography

Font Family: Poppins and Montserrat

Heading Font: Bold Poppins for all headings for better readability

Body Font: Montserrat will be used for body text, ensuring clarity and readability.
